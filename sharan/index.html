About Me

Sharan Choudhari, a young & accomplished Hindustani Classical Vocalist, carries numerous accolades & compositions in his repertoire, bestowed with Kiriya Sangeeth Samrat by Pt.Puttaraj Gawayi, Gadag at the age of 9. An avid music composer & director, has given one of his best compositions to a title song for a telefilm, "Galate Maduve", in which he has acted as a protagonist as well.

His musical journey began at a tender age of 4, when the famous clarionate artist Pandit G Ramulu, Raichuru, spotted the spark in him for music & guided him to pursue learning music. Later he took training under Vidwan Ningappa Dangi, Mudhol, for 15 long years. Gave his first classical vocal concert at the age of 5 years & 6 months. He has performed at national festivals, in different states of India & abroad, including Sweden & USA. Felicitated recently by Indian Heritage Society, Roanoke, Virginia State, USA.

He is a source of inspiration & guidance to young & talented artists, with his classes conducted at Sharan's Music Academy, R T Nagar, Bengaluru. His students span across India & abroad, some of them are playback singers & music composers.

Born in Raichuru, Karnataka state (India) on 23rd April-1984, completed his primary education from Jaya Vijaya primary school, Mudhol, later pursuing High School & College SRA Educational Institute, Banahatti. Secured merit seat in B.L.D.E.A's V.P. Dr. P.G. Halakatti College of Engineering and Technology & graduated as B.E (Automobile) from VTU, Belagavi. He currently works as a Project Leader in R&D department in one of the leading MNCs at Bengaluru.

He is a versatile exponent of a Variety of Hindustani music - Hindustani Classical Vocal, Semi Classical Vocal & Tarana, Hindi Bhajans & Thumri, Kannada Bhakti sangeeth - Vachana Gayana, Daasavani & Sugama Sangeetha.
