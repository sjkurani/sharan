The Magic Of Vachanas

By Sharan Choudhari July, 2010

First Music Album by Sharan Choudhari, it is a collection of 10 Vachans by, "Sage Basaveshwara", "Sage Allamaprabhu" and "Akkamahadevi". Music composition of these Vachanas is done in Hidustani Classical style.


Nee Baarade

By Sharan Choudhari July, 2010

Second album by Sharan Choudhari, It is collection of 9 Kannada bhavageethe. Songs written by famous lyricist Shrihari Dhoopad, Shri Sangamesh Koti, Prof.B.K Konnur & Subhash Yaranal. In this album Sharan tried to compose all songs in modern pattern using many live instruments with different concept. It made huge hit in online media.

Music programming done by Cajetan Dias. Renowned kannada playback singer Sangeeta Katti, Akanksha Badami, Singer Pooja Rao & Sanjay P G also sung in this album.
